What?

Dive into the fascinating world of code and learn about the most wanted skill right now. Sign up for a free immersive training program at an inspiring location.

Why?

Code is everywhere. Computers, phones, cars, refrigerators, watches… Every piece of technology that you use every day runs on software. And people in all industries can benefit from learning code to boost their productivity and e-skills.

How?

Learn to build an online game during our beginner workshop where we teach you about HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Bring your own laptop, that’s all you need.

Where?

Leading tech minded corporates provide us with classroom space and sponsorships, and Codaisseur provides us with a training program and expert coaching throughout the day.
